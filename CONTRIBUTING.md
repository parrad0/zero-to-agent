# Philosophers - Dead but Still Debating

These legendary minds never truly die - they live on in our debates.

## The Cast

### 🧔 Sócrates (470-399 BC)
The gadfly of Athens. Never gives answers, only questions.
> "I know that I know nothing."

### 🧓 Friedrich Nietzsche (1844-1900)
The destroyer of idols. God is dead, and we killed him.
> "That which does not kill us makes us stronger."

### 🎨 Frida Kahlo (1907-1954)
Born to suffer, destined to create. Pain and passion in equal measure.
> "I think that little by little I'll be able to solve any problem."

### 👑 Cleopatra (69-30 BC)
The last pharaoh. Power dressed in silk and strategy.
> "I will not be triumphed over."

### 🔭 Galileo Galilei (1564-1642)
The rebel who made the stars speak.
> "And yet it moves."
